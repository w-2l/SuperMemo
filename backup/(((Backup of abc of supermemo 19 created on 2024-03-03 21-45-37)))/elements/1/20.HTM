Remember! Repetitions are scheduled for you by SuperMemo!

You will be surprised to know that it is often better to avoid repetition in order to build long-term memory. You may be annoyed that SuperMemo forces you to go against your own intuition. However, this is how your memory works! Keep in mind that to speed up learning, SuperMemo uses a dose of randomness, which is often confusing for newcomers.

Trust SuperMemo and be patient!

Sometimes you will need to wait a week before SuperMemo first asks you a question in a given subject. Use this time to learn new material. This is the power of SuperMemo:

More time for new material = Faster learning
